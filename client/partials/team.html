Dog-Alicous Team

William

William is originally from Chicago and doesn't know why anyone would want to live anywhere else. When he isn't walking pups for Dog-alicious he is cheering for his Bears to beat the Packers! He is currently studying to be a web programmer when he isn't outside enjoying the city!

Jessica

Jessica recently moved to Chicago, but is originally from the south suburb of Calumet City. She has always loved dogs, their constant unconditional love, and their ability to put a smile on anyone's face no matter what! Outside of dog walking, Jessica works as a school social worker and a high school badminton coach. She enjoys spending time with her family and friends, yoga, dining out, and spending time at her family's lake house.

If she's not working out or playing with pups, she can be found hidden beneath a fort of blankets watching horror movies and happily cheating on her diet. Her favorite breeds of dogs are: "Every single dog in existence."

Lily

When she doesn't have a leash in her hand, you can catch her in a myriad of performance venues around Chicago. Between dog walking and dancing, she's always on the go!
